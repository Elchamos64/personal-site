About the page

The only action that you have to do here, it’s leave your ranking opinion between 1-5 and then leave a comment of why you’re rating it the way it is. And that’s it! Other people can read your comments but not interact or edit what you wrote. Since you don’t need to create an account, your comment will be anonymous and safe. By doing this I hope that the community can be honest about their experiences and can see the benefits from using a tool like this.
